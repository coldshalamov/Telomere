- `streaming-random-null-1k` is a deterministic pseudorandom control; any negative delta here would need investigation.
- `streaming-planted-density10` plants a small contiguous generated region, showing that sparse hits can still lose to container and literal overhead.
- `streaming-planted-density50` plants a larger contiguous generated region and records the break-even direction under the same v2 overhead.
