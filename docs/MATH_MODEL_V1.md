# Telomere V1 ‚Äî Mathematical Model

The economics companion to `SPEC_V1.md`. Everything here is derivable
with pencil and paper from one empirical assumption, and every number
carries its evidence class. Laptop runs validate laws and decode
behavior only ‚Äî never viability; viability claims live in the formulas.

## 1. The single assumption

**Uniform hash law.** For a seed expansion of S demanded bits,
`P(exact match) = 2^‚àíS` per (seed, key) trial, independent across
trials. This is the only empirical input. Everything else is counting.
(Validated at toy scale with real SHA-256 draws across keys and
positions; scaling validation is risk #1 in the risk register.)

## 2. Win-given-hit ‚âà 2 bits, at every scale

Record costs are within ~1 bit of Kraft-optimal (J3D1 Lotus), so the
number of distinct seeds whose records cost ‚â§ c bits roughly doubles per
extra bit of c. A window of W bits nets `W ‚àí c` bits when a seed of cost
c matches. Among compressive seeds (c < W), each extra bit of demanded
saving halves the eligible seed count while the per-seed hit probability
is constant ‚Äî so the *expected saving conditional on a hit* is the mean
of a geometric-like distribution concentrated at small savings:

    E[gain | hit] ‚âà Œ£_g g¬∑2^‚àíg / Œ£_g 2^‚àíg ‚âà 2 bits,

independent of W and of scale. Jackpots (a cheap seed reproducing a long
span) exist at their true exponential rarity and are included; they do
not move the mean. Consequence: **viability is a hit-DENSITY question,
never a hit-SIZE question.**

## 3. Per-window hit probability and the gap

A window of W bits is matched by a specific seed with probability 2^‚àíW.
Summing over all seeds whose records are compressive (cost < W) gives

    P(compressive hit on one window, one key) ‚âà 2^‚àígap,

where `gap = W ‚àí log2(#compressive seeds)` is set by record-format
overhead, not by search depth. **The gap is search-depth-invariant** ‚Äî
deeper search alone never closes it (the depth ceiling D*, ¬ß4). V1's
J3D1 single-block gap at B = 8 is ~10‚Äì13 bits depending on profile; the
exact per-arity table is generated by `costs.py` against the pinned
Lotus widths.

## 4. The depth ceiling D*

A seed whose record costs ‚â• the largest live window budget can never be
accepted. Therefore search past the costliest-useful seed is provably
worthless: depth saturates at D* and "how long to search" is a compute
knob only. Verified analytically and in the kernel sweeps (D48 ‚â° D96 at
B = 8).

## 5. Alphabet profiles (measured, real draws)

Two Kraft-complete assignments of the 6 codewords (spec ¬ß4). Salted
Monte-Carlo, ~2000 toy passes, real Bernoulli draws:
- *payback* (2-bit literal): shallower initial wrap, crosses sooner;
- *canonical* (2-bit arity-1 AND arity-2; 3-bit literal): earns 1.16√ó
  more per pass at every stage, starts ~12.5 points deeper, overtakes
  outright below ~0.69√ó of original.
One charged header bit selects; the choice is horizon policy, not math.

## 6. The freshness dichotomy (the central measured result)

Real-draw Monte Carlo (`monte_carlo_v1.py`), evolving-stream model:
- **Content-only dice** (no per-pass freshness): the machine is
  supply-bound ‚Äî it stalls ~13% above original size and never crosses.
  Each window owns one lifetime dice sequence; once spent, spent.
- **Fresh dice every pass**: crosses below original size (~pass 1000 at
  toy scale) and keeps earning without bound.

V1's normative model implements freshness where it is *decodable*:
- **Bundles**: key `H(seed, pass)` ‚Äî fresh every pass; decodable because
  the birth pass is inferred (spec ¬ß6; proven by construction 36/36).
- **Singles**: key `H(seed, original_slot)` ‚Äî birth-free and decodable
  forever, but one dice sequence per slot: a bounded opening channel
  that exhausts at D*.

## 7. The constant-N ledger (V1 proper)

V1 serializes ONCE, at the end ‚Äî so unlike the evolving-stream model
there is no per-pass re-wrap and no wrap refund dynamics. The exact
ledger:

    final_bits = Œ£_records cost(r) + Œ£_unclaimed (marker + B) + header
    net saving = Œ£_records [a¬∑B ‚àí cost(r)] ‚àí Œ£_unclaimed marker ‚àí header

with the trailing-literal stretch paying one 3-bit REMAINDER RUN marker
instead of per-block markers. Worst case (nothing ever claimed):
raw + 3 bits + header ‚Äî the pigeonhole obligation, met by construction.

Coverage grows with T (fresh adjacency via œÉ; fresh salt for bundles):
naively `1 ‚àí (1‚àí2^‚àígap)^T` ‚Üí full coverage as T ‚Üí ‚àû at ‚âà 2 bits per
record. **But the naive ledger omits a real cost ‚Äî see ¬ß7b.** What
survives unconditionally: V1 cannot bloat past raw + Œµ.

## 7b. The birth-information ledger (June 2026 ‚Äî load-bearing)

A bundle accepted at pass t wins E[win|hit] ‚âà 2 bits but the decoder
must learn its birth pass: ‚â• log2(t) bits of information that public
structure cannot fully supply at scale (a deterministic shuffle carries
zero bits; the file hash is 64 bits, not records √ó log2 T). Charging
this lower bound, **every configuration at every T is net negative on
arbitrary data**: short T loses to the literal wrap, long T loses to
birth entropy. Independently, Kraft/pigeonhole counting forbids the
naive result (almost-all files compressing 12% with bounded worst case
is impossible). Both arguments and the full parameter table:
`docs/GOLDEN_CONFIG.md` and `golden_break_even.py`. Singles dodge birth
entropy (slot-keyed, epoch-free) but are one-shot ‚Äî fresh dice and free
decode are mutually exclusive for singles. The unaided chance engine is
therefore a quantified shortfall, not a compressor; the distance to
break-even is ~50√ó hit density (p* ‚âà 0.19/window) at the Golden Config.

## 8. Unbounded recursion = layer stacking (open Q2)

Re-compressing records requires treating the emitted stream as a new
file (re-block, new N‚Ä≤, fresh coverage ‚Äî `STATE_MODEL_COMPARISON.md`
Design B+D). Decode needs no epochs (the layer boundary is the epoch,
charged in carriage); the open question is purely economic: per-layer
carriage (header + literal markers on the new blocking) vs per-layer
earnings (¬ß7 applied to the new file). Q2 is now priced negatively for
the unaided machine: constant-rate compounding would beat pigeonhole
(¬ß7b corollary), so recursion's value is contingent on the density lane
(`GOLDEN_CONFIG.md` ¬ß7). V1's unconditional headline is the bounded
ledger of ¬ß7 ‚Äî stated plainly.

## 9. Break-even arithmetic (why density is the whole game)

With ‚âà2-bit wins and per-literal markers, net compression of a span
population requires the fraction of spans finding compressive matches to
exceed roughly (marker bits)/(marker bits + mean win) ‚Äî about 3/4 at the
V1 alphabet (exact tables in `docs/archive/` legacy analyses).
Against the raw 2^‚àígap base rate this defines the density multiplier a
mechanism must supply (824√ó at bs=2 ‚Ä¶6144√ó at bs=8 in the legacy
flat-format analysis). The three independent levers: literal overhead
(format design), mean win (low-index seed bias), hit density (salts,
adjacency refresh, presets/dictionaries ‚Äî the natural-corpus lane).
